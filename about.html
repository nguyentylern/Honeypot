What is Honeypot?

In computer terminology a honeypot is a computer security mechanism set to detect, deflect,
or in some manner, counteract attempts at unauthorized use of information systems. Honeypot is
a website project by three students at Innovation Academy in Alpharetta, Georgia. Our project
was an assignment for the class, GT Block. GT Block is a required class for all freshman attending
Innovation Academy. In GT Block, you take 3 classes at once, IT, Healtcare, and Engineering. Our
project will be presented in the Innovation Academy (IA) project showcase. The IA showcase is a
collection of projects done by IA students to showcase their abilities and work throughout the year.
